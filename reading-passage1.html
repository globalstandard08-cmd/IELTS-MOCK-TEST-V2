Reading Test

You are about to start the Reading Test

The Reading test lasts 60 minutes. There are 3 sections with 40 questions in total. The timer will start as soon as you begin and will continue running until the time is up — it will not stop or reset when moving between sections.

Instructions

  • Answer all questions; there is no penalty for wrong answers.
  • Spelling and grammar must be correct.
  • Do not exceed the word limit given in the instructions.
  • You can move freely between sections during the test.
  • Select text in the passage to highlight it.
